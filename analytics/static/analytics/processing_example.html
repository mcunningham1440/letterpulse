How to Use this Feature

Processing splits your newsletters into sections that you specify. Each of those sections can be made up of one or more items, which are automatically detected by AI.

For example, if your newsletter consists of a Deep Dive section with an essay on one topic and a Quick Bites section with a series of 5 news links, the system would extract one item—the essay—from Deep Dive and 5 items—the 5 links—from Quick Bites.

Enter your newsletter's section titles (e.g. "Quick Bites", "Deep Dives") as the name so the AI knows what to extract from each post.

If the AI is having trouble finding a section or the items in it, you can add special instructions to help it out.
